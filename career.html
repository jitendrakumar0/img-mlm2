Work Culture
Demo. Digital embraces the culture of cohesiveness, appreciation and trust.
Culture sustains employee enthusiasm and therefore, at Demo. Digital we believe in promoting a culture of happiness and positivity. Happy employees mean better productivity at work. And when a business is more productive, it means the organisation is set to grow faster with outstanding performances. Positive organisational culture also leads to high motivation level. Strong and positive company culture is a powerful tool to attract the best talent and get the best work done.

To keep the spirit of our employees up and to maintain perfect work-life balance, we plan various activities, events and recreational trips regularly. It is our belief that work place should be like a second home to the employees and therefore, it is our responsibility to provide our employees with the best of facilities, support and comfort at work.

Demo. Values
Our values are the key lessons that help us do what we have set out to do.
Some of these we started with and some of these, we have learnt along the way. These are the path on which we travel every day. When faced with a doubt or a decision, these values are our friends - they guide us and take us along our journey. These values are what we all subscribe to.
We Respect each other
All of us comprise the Demo. Digital Universe. Whether distributor, co-worker, partner or customer, each one of us plays a unique and vital role. Each one is important and each of us in Demo. Digital live with the dignity that we deserve.

We demonstrate this respect in every possible way. How available we make ourselves to each other, the attention we have for each other during interactions, the directness and humility with which we engage and the courtesy and politeness that is recognised today as a symbol of the Demo. Digital Culture.

This respect is the cement that bonds us to one another.
We think, practice and behave with Honesty
Honesty starts with intent. At Demo. Digital, we have always strived to be an organisation that is recognised for its honesty and credibility. It is the foundation quality of trust for us.

Honesty at Demo. Digital begins with creating an environment of fairness and equality. An environment where we value the contributions of all and engage frankly with one another with complete integrity. Leading with personal example in all matters of ethics and honesty. Inspiring and demonstrating the courage to bring up matters that are crucial for the well-being and health of the organisation and its stakeholders. Even if it may not be a popular opinion. This helps us to be a real organisation that does the best for all, always.
We bring Passion to work
Our passion sets us apart from the world. We are driven intensely by our vision “to help people live a life of economic independence on their own terms”. To bring this vision to reality to more people in newer regions, we drive ourselves with passion every day.

As a community we are aligned by linking our purpose to the vision of the organisation. This means bringing our highest levels of energy to our work and to those around us. Building healthy and positive professional friendships that will help us move towards our goals. We share and communicate what we do with and bring newer innovations in our fields of work so that we improve the collective Demo. Digital experience all the time. This enthusiasm and spirit is visible in every aspect of our functioning. “Sochnay Ki Himmat” the inspiring guidance of our founder, leads us to launch breakthrough products, innovative services, and inventive business practices.
We commit to Growth
Growth is the measurement of our progress as individuals and as a community. While the growth of our business is the symbol of how we move forward, our individual attempt to keep advancing assures our personal growth. At Demo. Digital, we commit ourselves to this growth and make today better than yesterday and every tomorrow better than today.

We are conscious of who we are and what we seek to achieve. We work together with co-workers, managers and teams to understand what we need in order to learn and develop. This determination for self-renewal and betterment helps us accept challenges and become better professionals. This in turn leads to us anticipating and fulfilling the needs of our distributors, customers, co-workers and partners.
We discover Freedom & deliver Happiness!
At Demo. Digital, it is our purpose to deliver happiness to every individual who comes into contact with the organisation. When we deliver happiness, we help people to discover the freedom to be themselves. To be able to live life on their own terms. With that freedom, we empower people to help others.

Our way of life, then, is to create positively nourishing environment for people to work. Encouraging autonomy at the workplace, providing clarity of expectations and constructive feedback so that everybody is working with clarity and freedom to achieve and deliver happiness to all!

Employee Benefits Preposition
Family Mediclaim: Demo. Digital’s Family Mediclaim policy is an insurance plan under which the employees get a health cover of a certain amount for their family. As per the employee’s grade and company’s policy “Family Mediclaim” covers reimbursement of the cost of hospitalisation or domiciliary treatment of the employee, the spouse and 2 children.

Group Personal Accident Insurance: Our employees are our greatest asset and we value them and their closed ones and want to help them and their loved ones in case of any misfortune. For them, we have Group Personal Accident Insurance Cover. As per the employee’s grade and company’s policy this Accident provides insurance cover to the employee in the event of death or partial/permanent disablement due to accident/injury according to grade entitlement.

Product Benefit for Employees: Each employee can avail Demo. Digital products worth Rs. 1500/- per month after completing one month service in the company.

Merit Scholarship Scheme: Demo. Digital has introduced “Late Shri J.P Goyal & Smt. Adesh Goyal Memorial Scholarship” for our employee’s children who are pursuing or want to pursue Higher Sec. Education (XI and XII). Demo. Digital employees who have been working in the organisation for at least 2 years and more can get this scholarship for their children.

Marriage Gift: Marriage is a new beginning and in this special journey of life we have something special for our employees. On the marriage of our employees, we send a gift to make the celebration more memorable.

Loan & advance policy: We know that emergency and financial needs can be at the doorsteps without any prior notice and to deal with such situations we have a policy for loan and advance for our employees.

Wellness Program: Demo. Digital organizes Annual Health Camps to support employee’s health and well-being.

Suggestions Scheme: Demo. Digital appreciates suggestions given by the employees that lead towards better efficiency and help utilize the resources better and promotes the culture of working together as a family.

Meal Benefit: Demo. Digital provides Sodexo Meal Pass to employees worth Rs. 2000/- Per Month for purchase of food/meals and non-alcoholic beverages at Sodexo Merchant Outlets. This is subject to the paid days in the month and can be availed after credit of salary.
